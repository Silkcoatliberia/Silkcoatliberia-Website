About Silkcoat Liberia

Excellence in Paint Manufacturing Since 1985

Our Story

Silkcoat has been a leading name in the Turkish paint industry since 1985, known for innovation, quality, and environmental responsibility.

Now bringing our expertise to Liberia, we are committed to providing the same level of excellence that has made us a trusted brand across multiple markets. Our Liberian operations combine Turkish manufacturing excellence with local market understanding.

We understand the unique challenges of Liberia's tropical climate and have adapted our formulations to provide superior performance and durability in local conditions.

Our Mission

To provide premium quality paint solutions that enhance and protect spaces while contributing to the development of Liberia's construction and renovation sectors.

Our Vision

To become the leading paint brand in Liberia, known for quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction while maintaining our commitment to environmental sustainability.

Our Values

Quality, integrity, innovation, and customer focus drive everything we do. We believe in building lasting relationships through superior products and service.
