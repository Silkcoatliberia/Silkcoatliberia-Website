About Silkcoat Liberia

Excellence in Paint Manufacturing Since 1985

Our Story

Born from a Liberian vision for quality and durability, Silkcoat Paint Liberia is where global expertise meets local pride.

Our journey began when our Liberian CEO, Miss. Helena Smith, identified the need for world-class paints crafted for our nation's unique climate. She partnered with the Silkcoat Group—a global paint leader since 1993 renowned for its silk plaster, decorative coatings, and thermal insulation systems, exporting to 68 countries—to bring this vision to life.

Officially established in September 2025, we are more than a paint company. We are a local initiative committed to empowering Liberia by creating jobs and providing formulations specifically adapted to withstand tropical rains, intense sun, and humidity, ensuring superior protection and beauty for your home.

Silkcoat Paint Liberia is proudly Liberian, for Liberia, powered by a global standard of excellence.

Our Mission

To provide premium quality paint solutions that enhance and protect spaces while contributing to the development of Liberia's construction and renovation sectors.

Our Vision

To become the leading paint brand in Liberia, known for quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction while maintaining our commitment to environmental sustainability.

Our Values

Quality, integrity, innovation, and customer focus drive everything we do. We believe in building lasting relationships through superior products and service.
